# **App Name**: SwiftSend

## Core Features:

- Instant Quote Calculator: Calculates the shipping cost instantly based on destination, weight, and dimensions.
- Real-time Tracking: Track your package in real-time from pickup to delivery.
- AI-Powered Route Optimization Tool: Analyzes various routes using AI and other external datasets like weather conditions and traffic incidents to choose the fastest and most reliable routes. Generates ETAs (estimated time of arrival) and notifies users of delays if external circumstances could influence route times
- Service Selection: Choose from a variety of shipping options including express, standard, and economy.
- User Accounts: Allow businesses and individuals to make an account, manage addresses and view order history
- Integrated Logistics Management: Comprehensive logistics solutions for businesses, including warehousing and distribution.
- Customer Support Chat: Integrated customer service chat available 24/7 for support and queries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Corporate blue (#2962FF), reflecting trust and security.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E6EDFF), for a clean, minimalist feel.
- Accent color: Analogous bright violet (#7A47FF) used sparingly to draw attention to calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a modern and readable appearance.
- Use simple, line-style icons to represent services and steps in the process.
- Clean, grid-based layout with ample whitespace for a professional look.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance the user experience and convey dynamism.
